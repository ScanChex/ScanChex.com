Frequently Asked Questions

What equipment do I need to get started?

As ScanCheXtm is a web-based system, no special equipment is necessary. All that is required is a computer (for your dashboard), a printer (for code creation) and a smartphone (to scan) with GPS compatibility (iphone, Android or Blackberry).

What do I download to my computer ?

As noted above, since this a cloud or web-based system, nothing is required to be download to your local computer system, which means that you can have access anywhere you have a computer and internet connection. It also means that you dont need to worry about backups, files being compromised etc.

How much does this system cost?

ScanCheXtm was designed within the “pay as you go” concept; where your monthly fee is based on the number of codes you require, and not the amount of users like most other software packages. Click this link for more information on price packages-----

Can I access the software from another location?

Yes, you can have access anywhere you have a computer and internet connection.

Can I change the scan schedule or assigned employee at any time?

Yes you can .. see our help file here for instructions.

How is this different to GPS and other Asset tracking system?

ScanCheXtm is not meant to compete with GPS or expensive 'high-end' sophisticated monitoring systems. Usually these systems require expensive & proprietary scanning devices, along with a significant costs in terms of up-front fees for software and training of staff.

What is meant by geo-coded codes?

ScanCheXtm uses QR (quick response technology) codes that when placed at a location the unique longitude / latitude coordinates gives it a fixed position. This assigned code can then be configured and monitored at the discretion of the user.

Can I upgrade/get more codes for my account ?

Yes you can. By upgrading your package you automatically increase that amount of codes in your possession and your monthly payment date is now changed to the current plan chosen. We suggest that you make the change at your current monthly renewal date as we are unable to rebate or pro-rate past payments.

Can I downgrade the number of codes I have ?

Absolutely !

However, as a word of caution: if you have used all your codes, and you are reducing the total , then you should decide which ones you wanted to have deleted first, and then actually delete them, and then downgrade your plan.

We cannot be held responsible for any automatic deletion of assets which you may have assigned to codes.

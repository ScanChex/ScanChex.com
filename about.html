About ScanCheXTM, Inc

Veriscan Incorporated is an internet startup company based in Miami, Florida, USA and was founded in May 2012.

Veriscan's premier service, ScanCheXtm, is a subscription, cloud based 'Software As A Service' and specializes in giving its subscribers an inexpensive & easy to implement means to get Independent Verification ™ that your important assets and other critical items are being checked by your designated employees or contractors, on the frequency you have established for that item.

The service can be used for 5 categories of items:
  • Asset Inspections
  • Personnel Monitoring
  • Check Out - Check In
  • Service Module
  • Healthcare Module
ScanCheXtm is designed to be up and running in a matter of minutes without the need for specialized equipment and utilizes everyday smartphones as the scanning device.

The highest standards of programming and security have been employed and the service is constantly being improved as the company rolls out new features and improvements.

Veriscan, Inc is based in Miami, Florida, United States.
